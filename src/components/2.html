SHADOW A SCIENTIST


Shadow a Scientist is fiscally sponsored by The Hack Foundation (d.b.a. Hack Club), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499).


Shadow a Scientist is a registered non-profit company in South Africa dedicated to empowering undergraduate and honors students pursuing careers in medical sciences. We recognize the critical need for career guidance and mentorship in these fields and strive to provide students with unparalleled access to leading scientists and real-world experiences.


Developing Free and Accessible STEM Programs for Student Exploration and Experience

Lab Visits

Experience hands-on learning with guided lab visits, exploring real-world scientific research environments.

Talks

Engage with experts and expand your knowledge through insightful talks on various scientific topics.

Research Opportunities

Get involved in research and build your experience with unique scientific research opportunities.

Mentorship

Receive guidance and support through our mentorship programs to develop your skills and confidence.

Our Impact

Over 1500 attendees across three chapters—Wits, NWU (South Africa), and Kenya—with 40+ dedicated volunteers since 2022

HOW IT CAN BENEFIT YOU

Discover your passion

Explore a vast array of STEM fields, identify your unique talents and interests, and tailor your academic journey towards a career that truly resonates with you.

Unleash your inner scientist

Connect with leading scientists, glean insights from their inspiring journeys, and learn what it takes to succeed in diverse career paths.

Meet your STEM heroes

Immerse yourself in cutting-edge research labs and facilities and getting exposure to cutting-edge tools and methodology.
